Sample behavioral waveforms for design file "lpm_fifo0.tdf"

The following waveforms show the behavior of dcfifo megafunction for the chosen set of parameters in design "lpm_fifo0.tdf". The design "lpm_fifo0.tdf" has a depth of 256 words of 16 bits each. The fifo is in legacy synchronous mode. The data becomes available after 'rdreq' is asserted; 'rdreq' acts as a read request.
